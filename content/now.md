### Bonus

#### Current [papa doble](/articles/papa-doble) recipe

- measure into a shaker
  - 60ml rum (whichever I fancy and have on-hand)
  - 20ml (sometimes 15ml) Luxardo maraschino originale
  - 15ml (sometimes 20ml) grapefruit juice
  - 15ml lime juice
- shake with ice
- double strain into a nick and nora
- add no garnish
