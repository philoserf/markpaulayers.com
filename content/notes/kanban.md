### Fundamentals

1. Visualize work
2. Limit work-in-progress

### Method

1. Start where you are
2. Incremental, evolutionary change
3. Respect the current process, roles, responsibilities, and titles
4. Encourage acts of leadership at all levels

Cards are nouns Lanes are verbs
Cards are things Lanes are acivities

1. What type of work is it?
2. Where is it now?
3. Where did it come from?
4. Where will it go when I am done?

Are we a team? What is our common goal?

Work entering our process Work exiting our process

Look for queues, waiting Handoffs and not in our control
